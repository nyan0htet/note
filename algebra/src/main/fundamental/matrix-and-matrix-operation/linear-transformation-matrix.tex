\section{Linear Transformation of Matrix}

    v_1 \\ v_2 \\ \vdots \\ v_n
\end{bmatrix}$ ဖြစ်လာမည်။ 
\[
    \mtwotwo{1}{0}{0}{1}\stackrel{\m{A}}{\longrightarrow}\mtwotwo{2.1}{0.2}{0.3}{1.2}
\]
